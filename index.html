Welcome to the Last Of The Walking Dead RP

Last of The Walking Dead RP takes place in a crossover universe between The Walking Dead and The Last of Us. Along with the expanded cast comes greater risks and threats.

Roleplayers will find themselves in a world that is never safe, fourteen years after the apocalypse began.

Right now, we are a small, tightknit group of experienced roleplayers and fanfiction writers, exploring an alternate universe that will both be exciting and new.
